How it works
  • Paid proportional — partial payers participate with what they actually paid.
  • Fully paid only — member must be fully paid to participate; basis is still paid money.
  • Distributing profit does not change the investment’s recorded profit generated; it reduces the remaining pool for members.

If Investment-scoped profit is enabled under Settings, only share subscriptions linked to this investment are in the denominator. Otherwise, global pool rules apply (subscriptions + unlinked payments as configured).
