How Estithmar operates

Members join the system, purchase investment shares, and pay either in full or on an installment plan. When a member has fully paid their subscribed share value, the system confirms ownership and can issue a share certificate.

Collected funds are pooled and allocated into approved investment projects in categories such as Real Estate, Medical, Trading, Services, Partnership, Agriculture, and Transport.

When projects generate profit, distributions split the pool by real money paid (contribution transactions): ownership % = member paid ÷ total paid. Subscribed amounts are never used as the basis. Policies only gate who participates (paid proportional vs fully paid only).
