Headline totals (members, agents, collected, invested, profit, projects) are on the Dashboard. The five core report areas below cover members, agents, contributions, investments, and profit.

Business model alignment. The platform implements community investment flows: member → agent assignment → share subscription (full or installments) → contribution receipts → project/investment deployment → profit on paid-eligible basis → distribution batches → certificates when fully paid and confirmed. See Community investment model for narrative and live metrics.
