Basis of profit sharing

Ownership % for each active member = eligible paid amount ÷ sum of eligible paid amounts across all participating members. Member profit = ownership % × profit pool.

Eligible paid amounts use actual contributions recorded against share subscriptions (and, in global pool mode, unlinked member contributions), never the promised subscription total — unless a subscription uses the fully paid only policy, in which case nothing counts until the subscription is fully paid.

  • Verified-only basis: {{ 'On — only verified payments count.' if profit_basis_verified else 'Off — all recorded payments count.' }}
  • Fully paid only (org-wide): {{ 'On — only Fully Paid subscriptions participate; unlinked contributions excluded from global pool.' if settings.get_extra().get('profit_global_fully_paid_only') else 'Off — per-subscription policy applies (paid proportional or fully paid only).' }}
  • Investment-scoped pool: {{ 'On — only subscriptions linked to the selected investment participate.' if profit_investment_scoped else 'Off — organization-wide eligible pool for the chosen investment vehicle context.' }}
